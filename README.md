# Noitreign

Time-pressure mod for [Noita](https://noitagame.com/) inspired by
Elden Ring: Nightreign.

A countdown timer per biome forces you forward. When it expires, the
Curse of Putterers will fall upon the player, dealing damage as the
tock ticks.

Because puttering is power in Noita, the Gods have seen fit to offer
you some additional benefits when you simply make it to the next Holy
Mountain.

## Installation

Copy this folder into your Noita mods directory, then enable
**Noitreign** from the in-game mods menu.

## Details

You start each biome with 200 seconds (or 3.33 minutes, adjustable in
the settings). When the clock expires you take damage on each tick
(which is 2 seconds, tock).

Additional time (+90 seconds) is granted when you move laterally
through biomes. For example, if you enter the Fungal Caverns from the
Coal Pits +90. Returning to a previously seen biome does not grant you
time after the first visit.

The Holy Mountains are safe -- your timer stops, and you can twiddle
with wands at your leisure. If you collapse the Holy Mountain, it is
no longer safe, but traditional Holy Mountain hijinx can also provide
you some respite from the clock if you can keep it from collapsing.

When you enter a Holy Mountain: move a little to the right! There were
a lot of edge case to figure out about how when a player enters a Holy
Mountain, so the leftmost area is **not** safe from the Curse of Those
Who Putter.

Inside the Holy Mountain: an additional perk will drop and you will be
given a bonus in Max HP (default 40, adjustable in the settings). Take
the perk if you like, or leave it if you don't.

Back trackers, be warned! Forward is the path in Noitreign, and the
way back only leads to Putter.
